Barreirinhas

Barreirinhas is a municipality in the state of Maranhão in the Northeast region of Brazil . [1] [2] [3] [4]

The municipality contains part of the 1,535,310 hectares (3,793,800 acres) Upaon-Açu/Miritiba/Alto Preguiças Environmental Protection Area , created in 1992. [5]

Transport

The locality is served by the Barreirinhas Airport .
