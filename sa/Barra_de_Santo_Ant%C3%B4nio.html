Barra de Santo Antônio

Barra de Santo Antônio is a municipality in the state of Alagoas in Brazil . The population is 15,742 (2015 est.) in an area of 138.43 km². [1] The elevation is 10 m. It is situated on the Atlantic coast , northeast of Maceió .
