Alta Floresta

Alta Floresta (Portuguese for "High Forest") is a municipality in Mato Grosso , Brazil . It is located at around 9°53′10″S 56°5′14″W ﻿ / ﻿ 9.88611°S 56.08722°W ﻿ / -9.88611; -56.08722 Coordinates : 9°53′10″S 56°5′14″W ﻿ / ﻿ 9.88611°S 56.08722°W ﻿ / -9.88611; -56.08722 .

The municipality is served by Piloto Osvaldo Marques Dias Airport .

The municipality contains a small part of the 184,900 hectares (457,000 acres) Cristalino State Park , created in 2001. [1]
