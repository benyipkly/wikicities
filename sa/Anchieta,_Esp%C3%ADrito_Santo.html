Anchieta, Espírito Santo

Anchieta is a municipality in the Brazilian state of Espírito Santo . Its population was 27,624 in 2015 and its area is 412 km². [1] Its average elevation is 2m above sea level.

Formerly known as Reritiba , the city was renamed after the Jesuit Spanish missionary and saint José de Anchieta (1534-1597).
